ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Kristina!

I am a student at Indiana University Bloomington studying Environmental Management. I am an avid traveler and I love discovering new places. I created this website to share photos from my travels and celebrate the memories I cherish most. As someone who finds meaning in preserving moments, I channel my creativity through photography, journaling, and scrapbooking.

I also host a college radio show called Snail Mail Radio as one of my primary creative outlets. I share a wide range of music that gives me joy and connects me with my listeners. You can listen to recordings of my shows by clicking here.

I’m driven by a desire to connect, create, and inspire positive change. Let’s explore together!
